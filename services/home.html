Technology Help That Speaks Your Language

You shouldn't need a degree in computing to get your technology working properly. We provide straightforward, affordable IT support for home users — covering everything from setting up a brand-new device to keeping your family protected online. All of our services are delivered remotely, so there's no waiting for an engineer to visit and no need to unplug anything.

💻 New Devices & Setup

Just bought a new laptop or PC? We'll take the stress out of getting started. Via a secure screen-share session, we walk you through the full setup — creating your user account, signing in to your email, installing the apps you need, configuring security settings and transferring your important files and photos from your old device. Everything is done remotely, and you're in control the whole time.

  • Windows and macOS setup and personalisation
  • Account creation and email configuration
  • Data transfer from your old device
  • Essential software installation
  • Printer and peripheral setup guidance

Computer running slowly? Getting strange pop-ups? Can't install an update? We connect remotely to your device, find the cause and fix it — while you watch. No need to take your laptop to a shop or wait days for it to come back. Most software issues are sorted in a single session.

  • Slow performance diagnosis and repair
  • Virus and malware removal
  • Software crashes and error messages
  • Windows and macOS update issues
  • Application conflicts and uninstalls

  • Safe-browsing tools and ad-blocker configuration
  • Home Wi-Fi security check — no more default passwords
