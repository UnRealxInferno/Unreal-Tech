Microsoft 365 Migration & Management

Microsoft 365 is the backbone of modern small business productivity — email, documents, spreadsheets, video calls, and team collaboration in one integrated platform. If you're still running on-premises email servers, an ageing file server, or a patchwork of different tools, migrating to Microsoft 365 can dramatically simplify your IT and reduce ongoing costs.

We handle the entire migration process — from planning and user account setup to data transfer, DNS configuration and post-migration training — with no data loss and minimal disruption to your team. Once you're on Microsoft 365, we can manage your licences, user accounts and security configuration on an ongoing basis.

Cloud Backup & Disaster Recovery

Data loss is one of the most devastating things that can happen to a business. Whether caused by ransomware, accidental deletion, hardware failure or a physical disaster, losing critical business data can be irreversible without proper backups in place.

We implement automated, tested cloud backup solutions that protect your files, emails and business data. Critically, we also test recoveries regularly — a backup that has never been tested is a backup you can't rely on. Our disaster recovery planning ensures you know exactly how to restore operations quickly if the worst happens.

  • Automated daily or real-time cloud backup
  • Microsoft 365 backup (email, SharePoint, OneDrive, Teams)
  • Offsite and air-gapped backup for ransomware resilience
  • Recovery time objective (RTO) planning and testing
  • Business continuity documentation

Wi-Fi Design & Installation

Poor Wi-Fi in the office isn't just annoying — it affects productivity, causes dropped calls and slows down cloud applications. We design and install enterprise-grade wireless networks that provide reliable, fast coverage across your entire premises. We configure separate SSIDs for staff, guests and IoT devices, implement appropriate security policies, and ensure your network can scale as your business grows.

Network Cabling & Switch Configuration

A well-structured physical network is the foundation of reliable IT. We install and terminate structured cabling (Cat5e / Cat6), configure managed switches with VLANs and QoS settings, and ensure your network topology is documented and maintainable. Whether you're fitting out a new office or untangling years of spaghetti cables, we deliver a clean, professional result.

VPN for Remote Workers

Giving remote staff secure access to office resources — file servers, printers, internal applications — requires a properly configured VPN. We deploy and manage business VPN solutions that are simple for staff to use but robust in their security, ensuring remote work doesn't create vulnerabilities in your network perimeter.
