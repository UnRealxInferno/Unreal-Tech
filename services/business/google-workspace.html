Why Google Workspace?

Google Workspace gives your business the same productivity and collaboration tools used by millions of organisations worldwide — on your own domain, in the cloud, accessible from any device. No servers to maintain, no complex licensing to manage, and no expensive upfront costs.

Tired of Outlook going down at the wrong moment, OneDrive sync errors that delete your work, or paying for a Microsoft 365 licence you barely use? Google Workspace is built differently — simpler, more reliable, and designed to work from any browser on any device without constant updates breaking things.

Whether you're a sole trader who wants a professional you@yourbusiness.co.uk email address, a home business getting serious about collaboration, or a growing SMB that needs reliable shared drives and video meetings — Google Workspace scales with you.

Setup & Migration

We handle the full process so you don't have to worry about it:

  • Domain verification and DNS configuration (MX, SPF, DKIM, DMARC)
  • Account creation and licence management
  • Migration of existing emails, contacts and calendar events from M365, legacy providers or on-premise Exchange
  • Shared Drive and organisational unit setup
  • Security hardening — 2FA enforcement, session controls, app access policies

Ongoing Management

As part of our Managed IT service, we take care of your Google Workspace admin on an ongoing basis — adding new users when you hire, removing leavers, managing permissions, monitoring licence costs, and acting as your helpdesk for any Google Workspace queries.

Training & Onboarding

We offer practical, jargon-free remote training sessions for your team covering Gmail, Drive, Docs, Meet, Calendar and any other tools your team will use. We focus on the workflows that matter to your business, not generic tutorials.
