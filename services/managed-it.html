What Is a Managed IT Service?

A Managed Service Provider (MSP) takes responsibility for your IT systems for a fixed, predictable monthly fee. Instead of waiting for something to break and then paying for emergency support, a managed IT service means issues are detected and resolved proactively — often before they affect your team at all.

For small businesses, outsourcing IT to an MSP is typically more cost-effective than hiring an in-house IT team, and more comprehensive than relying on ad-hoc support. You get enterprise-grade monitoring, security and helpdesk access without the enterprise price tag.

Remote Monitoring & Management (RMM)

Our monitoring tools watch over every device in your business 24 hours a day. We receive automated alerts for hardware failures, unusual activity, disk space warnings, failed backups and security incidents — and we act on them before they cause downtime for your staff.

  • Real-time alerts for hardware and software faults
  • Automated performance monitoring (CPU, RAM, disk)
  • Failed backup detection and remediation
  • Security event monitoring and response

Helpdesk Support

When your team has an IT problem, they need help fast. Our helpdesk provides remote support via phone, email and remote access tools — typically resolving issues within one business hour. For problems that require an on-site visit, we'll dispatch an engineer to you.

Every ticket is logged, tracked and resolved systematically so nothing falls through the cracks.

Patch Management & Asset Inventory

Unpatched software is one of the leading causes of security breaches. We automate patch management across all your Windows, macOS and third-party applications, ensuring your systems are always up to date without disrupting working hours. We also maintain a full, accurate inventory of all hardware and software assets in your business.

Cloud Services & Staff Onboarding

We manage your Microsoft 365 tenancy — user accounts, licences, email, Teams and SharePoint — and handle onboarding and offboarding of staff so devices and accounts are provisioned or decommissioned securely every time.
