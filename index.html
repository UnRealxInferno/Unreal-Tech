About Us

Passionate About Technology, Focused on People

Unreal Tech was founded on a simple belief: great IT support should be accessible to everyone—not just large enterprises. Whether you're a home user, running a business from your spare room, or a small-to-medium company starting to expand, you deserve reliable technology and expert help when things go wrong.

As a Google-focused managed service provider, we specialise in rolling out and supporting Google Workspace so that growing teams can collaborate from day one. Our engineers bring years of hands-on experience across consumer electronics, enterprise infrastructure, and cybersecurity, and we invest in continuous training to stay ahead of the latest challenges.

Based locally, we pride ourselves on building long-term relationships with our customers—not just fixing tickets and moving on. If your business is scaling up and you need an IT partner who scales with you, we'd love to chat.
