Why We Started

Tired of MSPs That Overcharge, Underdeliver — and Burn Out Their Own People

The managed IT industry has a problem. Too many providers win contracts by promising the world, then deliver the bare minimum. Monthly reports full of graphs nobody reads. Engineers stretched so thin they never actually fix anything properly. Customers paying premium prices for a service that feels like an afterthought.

We've seen it from the inside. Engineers working in poor conditions — minimal training, high pressure, no recognition — burning out and leaving the industry. Meanwhile customers are left with rotating helpdesk staff who don't know their business, their systems, or their name.

Unreal Tech was started to prove it doesn't have to be that way.

We built this company around a simple idea: if you look after your engineers and remember there's a person on the other end of every support ticket, everything else follows. Engineers who are treated well do better work. Customers who feel known and valued don't look elsewhere. Problems get solved properly the first time, because the person fixing it actually cares.

We keep our pricing honest and our work transparent. If something can't be fixed, we'll say so. If something doesn't need to be fixed, we'll tell you that too. We don't upsell, we don't overcomplicate, and we don't hide behind jargon.

Whether you're a home user who just needs their laptop sorting, or a small business that wants to stop worrying about IT — you'll always speak to a real person who knows your name.

People First

Security Built In

Every service we deliver has security thinking baked in from the start — not bolted on at the end when something's already gone wrong.
